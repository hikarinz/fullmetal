Summary of the story

Brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric lose their mother at a young age, and attempt to bring her back to life using alchemy. As a result of breaking the greatest taboo in alchemy, human transmutation, Edward loses an arm and a leg, while his younger brother loses his whole body. After the traumatic experience Edward vows to return Alphonse's body and becomes a state alchemist in order to find clues about the Philosopher's stone. The legendary stone is rumoured to enhance an alchemist's abilities and more importantly, ignore the laws of equivalent change.
